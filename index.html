Navitha Sri Kamisetty is an aspiring UI/UX Designer and Front-End Developer from Chirala, Andhra Pradesh, India, with a strong passion for building clean, user-friendly digital experiences. She graduated in 2023 with a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Engineering from St. Ann’s College of Engineering and Technology.

Her creative journey in tech began during her academic years, and she has since been actively involved in web design and development projects — including internships at InfoCepts, KPMG, and Suvidha Foundation, where she worked on real-time data and cloud engineering tasks using tools like AWS, Power BI, and SQL.

Navitha enjoys transforming ideas into functional and visually appealing interfaces. Her interest extends to designing engaging front-end interfaces, solving logical problems with Python, and creating insightful dashboards with SQL and Power BI.

She’s constantly learning, whether through hands-on projects or her ongoing training at NxtWave CCBP 4.0. When she's not coding or designing, you’ll find her exploring design inspiration online, watching web development tutorials, or spending quality time with her family.

Currently, she is open to exciting opportunities where she can contribute, learn, and grow as a creative front-end developer or UI/UX designer.
